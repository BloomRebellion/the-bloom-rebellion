⚖️ Know Your Rights

If you're worried about CPS or custody

Cannabis use — even where it's fully legal — can still come up in custody disputes or child welfare investigations. Laws and enforcement vary enormously by state, and having accurate, current information matters. This isn't legal advice, and nothing here should replace speaking with an actual attorney about your specific situation.

Family Law & Cannabis Alliance (FLCA)

A nonprofit offering free, state-specific legal information for parents navigating cannabis use and family court or CPS involvement.

Visit flcalliance.org

🤢 Hyperemesis Gravidarum

You are not imagining it, and you are not alone

Hyperemesis Gravidarum (HG) is a severe, potentially dangerous form of pregnancy nausea and vomiting — far beyond typical morning sickness. For decades, many women with HG were dismissed by doctors. Research has only recently begun identifying its physiological causes.

HER Foundation

The world's largest network of HG survivors and experts, offering education, symptom-tracking tools, provider referrals, and community support.

Visit hyperemesis.org
