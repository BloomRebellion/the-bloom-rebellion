🌍 Organizations Doing the Work

National groups worth knowing

These are established, verified organizations working on different parts of cannabis reform and justice. We'll keep adding to this list as the archive grows.

NORML

The oldest and largest cannabis reform organization in the U.S., founded in 1970. NORML advocates for ending cannabis prohibition and lobbies state and federal legislators on reform.

Visit norml.org

Last Prisoner Project

A nonprofit dedicated to cannabis criminal justice reform — working to release incarcerated cannabis prisoners, clear records, and support reentry.

Visit lastprisonerproject.org

Family Law & Cannabis Alliance (FLCA)

Free, state-specific legal information for parents navigating cannabis use alongside family court or CPS involvement. Also listed on our Mothers page.

Visit flcalliance.org
